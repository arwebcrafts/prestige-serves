Order Skip Trace

Fast. Discreet. Court-ready.

When service is time-sensitive, an outdated address can cost weeks. Our skip tracing service helps identify current location information and the most up-to-date agent-for-service details so you can move your case forward with confidence.

What We Provide

Address Research (Individuals)

We help locate the most current address information available for individuals who are difficult to serve or have recently moved.

Agent-for-Service Verification (Businesses)

We can confirm the current registered agent / agent for service and business address details to support lawful service and proper filing.

Case-Ready Delivery

Results are provided in a clear, usable format so you can proceed with service attempts quickly and document your due diligence where appropriate.

When Skip Tracing Is Recommended

  • Mail is returned or the address is incomplete
  • Prior service attempts were unsuccessful
  • The defendant recently moved or is avoiding service
  • A business address is outdated or the registered agent changed
  • You need the most current agent-for-service information before service

What We Need from You

  • Full legal name (and known aliases, if applicable)
  • Last known address / city
  • Date of birth (if available)
  • Phone number or email (if available)
  • Business name + state of registration (for businesses)
  • Any deadline or hearing date

The more you provide, the faster and more accurate the result.
